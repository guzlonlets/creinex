Creinex Management

Creinex Management is a dynamic private limited company found by a black female in 2015, it is based in Boshkop Pretoria East operating in the field of Bulk Bags Manufacturing and Supply Services of Various Products.

Bulk Bags are Known as Flexible intermediate Bulk Containers,(FIBC) OR Sacks OR Jumbo Bags Using weaved virgin polypropylene fabric of different types of strength and type as per application and Design and According to SANS.
